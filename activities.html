Co-Curricular Activities

We believe every child has a talent. Our activities programme helps them find it and shine.

⚽

Sports

Football, volleyball, athletics, and netball keep our learners active, fit, and team-spirited. We participate in inter-school competitions and local sports days.

🎵

Music & Choir

From singing to percussion, our music programme nurtures creativity and expression. Our choir participates in county-level festivals and school events.

🎭

Drama & Theatre

Learners build confidence, communication, and creativity through storytelling, role-play, and theatrical performances at school events.

🎨

Art & Craft

Creative arts sessions allow learners to express themselves through drawing, painting, model-making, and design projects aligned with the CBC creative arts strand.

📚

Reading Club

Our library-based reading club encourages a love for books and storytelling. Weekly reading sessions improve vocabulary, comprehension, and imagination.

🌱

Gardening & Agriculture

Hands-on gardening activities connect learners to nature, teach responsibility, and reinforce science and agriculture learning in the CBC curriculum.

💻

Computer Club

Early digital skills are developed through our computer club — covering typing, basic coding, and digital literacy for the modern world.

🙏

Clubs & Societies

Debate club, environmental club, scouts, and leadership programmes help learners develop character, voice, and community responsibility.

School Events

A year full of memorable moments that celebrate achievement, creativity, and community.

🏆

Annual Sports Day

Our biggest outdoor celebration! Learners compete in track, field, and team sports. A spectacular day of fun, competition, and school spirit shared with families.

🎤

Cultural Day

Learners showcase Kenya's rich cultural diversity through traditional music, dance, costumes, and food — celebrating our heritage with pride and joy.

🎓

Graduation Ceremony

We celebrate our graduating learners with a formal ceremony that marks their achievements and transition to the next chapter of their academic journey.

🚌

Educational Trips

Annual excursions to museums, national parks, and community institutions bring classroom learning to life and broaden horizons beyond the school compound.
