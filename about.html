Our Story

Sanduk Little Angels Academy Utawala was founded with a singular purpose: to provide the children of Utawala and its surroundings with access to quality, affordable, and holistic education. What began as a small community school has grown into a thriving academic institution serving hundreds of families.

Over the years, we have invested in modern classrooms, digital learning tools, and a team of highly qualified educators who are passionate about every child's growth. Our school has consistently produced well-rounded graduates who go on to excel in secondary schools and beyond.

Today, Sanduk Little Angels Academy stands as a beacon of hope and excellence in Utawala — a place where every child is seen, valued, and empowered to shine.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide inclusive, quality, and competency-based education that empowers every learner to discover their unique potential, develop strong moral values, and become a confident contributor to society and the nation of Kenya.

We achieve this through dedicated teaching, a safe learning environment, parental partnership, and continuous improvement in all we do.

🌟 Our Vision

To be the leading primary school in Utawala and Machakos County — renowned for nurturing brilliant, responsible, and globally competitive young citizens who are rooted in African values and ready for the world.

We envision a future where every child who walks through our gates walks out with confidence, character, and capability.
