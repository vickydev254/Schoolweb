Who We Are

Sanduk Little Angels Academy Utawala is a trusted primary school located in the heart of Utawala, Machakos County, Kenya. We are committed to providing every child with a world-class education rooted in Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

Our school is more than classrooms — it is a community of passionate teachers, supportive parents, and bright, curious learners. We believe that when a child feels loved and safe, they learn best.

From PP1 through Grade 9, our learners are guided, challenged, and celebrated every step of the way. Join us and give your child the bright future they deserve.
