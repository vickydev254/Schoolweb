Admission Process

A simple, welcoming process to get your child started at Sanduk Little Angels.

1

Contact the School

Call or visit us to express interest and get information about available spaces for your child's grade level.

2

School Tour & Meeting

We invite you and your child for a guided tour of the school. Meet the teachers, see the classrooms, and ask any questions you may have.

3

Submit Application Form

Complete and submit the admission application form below (or collect a printed copy from our office) along with the required documents.

4

Receive Offer Letter

Our office will review your application and contact you within 5 working days with an admission decision and next steps.

5

Pay & Confirm Enrolment

Confirm your child's place by paying the registration fee. You'll receive a welcome pack and your child's class details.

Requirements

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓ Child's Birth Certificate (original + copy)
  • ✓ 2 recent passport photos of the child
  • ✓ Parent/Guardian National ID (copy)
  • ✓ Previous school report card (for Grade 2 and above)
  • ✓ Transfer/Leaving Certificate from previous school (if transferring)
  • ✓ Immunisation card / Health records

👶 Age Requirements

  • PP1 Age 4–5 years
  • PP2 Age 5–6 years
  • Grade 1 Age 6+ years
  • Grade 2–9 As per CBC progression
