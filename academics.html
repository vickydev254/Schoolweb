The CBC Curriculum

At Sanduk Little Angels Academy, we fully implement Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), a modern, learner-centred approach that focuses on developing practical skills, values, and attitudes — not just content knowledge.

The CBC framework shifts the focus from rote learning to hands-on, inquiry-based, and collaborative learning. Our teachers are trained to guide each learner at their own pace, celebrating effort, curiosity, and individual strengths.

Through project-based learning, digital tools, and community-connected activities, our pupils are equipped not just to pass examinations, but to think critically and thrive in real life.

Our Teaching Approach

Modern, learner-centred methods that make education exciting and effective.

🔍

Inquiry-Based Learning

We encourage learners to ask questions, investigate, and discover answers — developing curiosity and independent thinking skills that last a lifetime.

💻

Digital Learning

Classrooms are equipped with digital tools to enhance lessons. Learners engage with educational content through technology-enhanced activities.
